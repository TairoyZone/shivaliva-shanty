# Devlog #3: Stardust Wells, Cut Gems, and a Mining glow-up ✨

Hey friends. Quiet, heads-down week: no new systems, no big promises, just a proper coat of paint on two of the mini-games. Honestly my favorite kind of work. Still pre-alpha, but pre-alpha can still be pretty. 🌌

**Gem Drop is now a Stardust Well.** 🌌
The board used to be a plain little grid, and it bugged me. So I rebuilt it as a starlit shaft plunging down into the Stardust, framed in warm brass. The big fix: the see-saw paddles were flat bars, and the round coins would teeter on them all wobbly, like they were about to bail. They're proper little CUPS now, so the coins nestle in and rest dead centre, calm as you like. I gave the coins a personality too: warm gold for you, ruby for your rival, so one glance tells you whose is whose, no squinting. And there's a real drop preview at last. Hover a chute and a half-see-through coin spins right where it'll land while the whole lane lights up, so you can read the path down before you commit. No more praying. 🪙

**Mining got a top-to-bottom art pass.** ⛏️
The common ground used to be vague colored blocks, let's be honest. Now it actually looks like stone, soil, sand, gravel and clay. Earthy, matte, humble dirt. And because the dirt finally looks like dirt, the valuable ore reads as shiny CUT GEMS that pop right out of the muck. Treasure looking like treasure. Wild concept, I know. 💎 The special tools got some love as well. Pickaxe, Drill, Cave-In, Tremor, and the little Seepage bug are all colour-coded with clear symbols now, and when you line one up it pops its name plus a one-line note on what it actually does. No more memorizing mystery icons. Oh, and a 2x2 you can't rotate now gives a little flash to say "nope, blocked," instead of silently doing nothing and making you feel like the game hated you. That one quietly drove me up the wall, so it's gone.

That's the update. Honest dirt, shiny gems, and coins that finally sit still. Go drop some coins and dig up some gems, and tell me what feels off. I'm always listening. 🛠️

Trojan Bulldog 🐶
