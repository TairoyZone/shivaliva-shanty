# Devlog #4: A Gym, a Jungle, and Fights That Finally Have Stakes 🥊

Hey friends. Bigger swing this time. The last couple of updates were paint and polish, but this one adds real systems, the kind that give the island a backbone. Still pre-alpha, still free to poke at, but it grew some teeth this week. 🌙

**Jade's hut is now the Cradle Gym.** 🥊
Mossy Jade used to run a little healer's hut. She's still there, and she'll still patch you up for free after a scrap, but the place is a proper fighting gym now, and Hollow Ellison runs the mats. The headline is the Gym Ladder: spar the whole town one rung at a time, each opponent a little tougher than the last, all the way up to Ellison himself at the top. Climb it clean and you're crowned Gym Champion, trophy and all. It's friendly sparring, so nobody gets hurt. It's just you, proving you belong.

**Past the forest, the jungle has gone wild.** 🦁
There's a new place to test that belt: the Jungle Ordeal. A hand-built maze with five beasts lurking in it. A Maned Lion, a Silverback Gorilla, a Charging Rhino, a Grizzled Bear, and at the very end, the Jungle King. Beat them in order to cut a path to the heart of it. Conquer the whole thing and you earn the Badge of Honour, and the entire town's respect goes up a notch. This one is NOT friendly sparring. These fights bite.

**Which brings me to the big one: fighting has stakes now.** 💢
Your fighter has health, and it carries between fights. Lose a serious bout and you take a hit. Walk into the next one worn down and your Skirmish board starts the round half-buried in rubble, up to 80% buried if you're completely floored. It's still playable at the bottom, I made sure of that, but it's a real handicap. The cure is simple, and it's the whole point: go see Jade, rest up, walk back in fresh. Fight smart, fight rested. 🛌

**The islands breathe now: day and night.** 🌅
This is my favorite one. There's a full day-and-night cycle across the whole overworld. The world warms at dusk, sinks into deep blue at night, a sun arcs overhead through the day, and a crescent moon and the stardust stars take the night shift. There's a little brass clock tucked in the top-right corner wherever you wander, a sun (or moon) sliding along its arc, so you can always read the hour at a glance. The islanders even greet you to the actual time of day.

**A few smaller things I'm happy about.** 🔑
Your standing with the cast now reads in plain words right on your Profile. Stranger, Acquaintance, Friend, Confidant, and the sour end of it too if you've burned someone. The old hearts are gone. Locked doors and earned keys gate the Mine, the Grove, and the Jungle now, so the world opens up as you prove yourself instead of all at once. And here's the bit I like: the shop owners won't just hand you a job. Beat Cinder Troy at the gym to earn the mining work and its Mine Key, beat Cogwise Godfrey for the lumber work and its Grove Key, and become Gym Champion for the Jungle Key. You have to show you can hold your own before they'll take you on. The parlor games, poker and Gem Drop, are cash tables only now, so a seat at one means something. Oh, and one for the woodcutters: tiles hold still for the break animation before they fall, like a proper old swordfight, instead of snapping down the instant something clears.

That's the update. The island's got a gym, a jungle, a clock, and consequences. Go climb the ladder, go pick a fight with a bear, and come tell me how it feels. I'm always listening. 🛠️

Trojan Bulldog 🐶
