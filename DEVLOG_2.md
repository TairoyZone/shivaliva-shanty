# Devlog #2: It plays right on your phone now 📱

Since launch, the number one thing folks asked for was a Shivaliva Shanty that actually feels good on a phone in the browser. So that is what this whole update is about. Pull it up on your phone, turn it sideways, and dig in.

## 📱 Built for touch

- 🕹️ **Thumb joysticks.** Mining and Skirmish dropped the fiddly arrow buttons for a proper thumb stick. In Skirmish you pull the stick down to soft drop, just like mobile Tetris.
- 🔍 **Pinch to zoom.** The islands and the Gem Drop table read tiny on a small screen, so now you can pinch to zoom in and slide around to see what you are doing.
- 🔠 **Bigger, readable text.** The chat box, the typing field, and every nametag scale up on touch, so you are not squinting at the crew.
- ⚡ **Smoother play.** Mining and Gem Drop were stuttering on phones. I tracked down why and they run clean now.

## 🛠️ Fixes

- 🧩 **Squashed a nasty Skirmish freeze.** The board could lock up mid-match right after a line clear. It is gone for good, with a test in place so it stays gone.
- 💬 **The cast is back.** Chatting with the crew works again in the browser build. They do need a live connection to talk back, so play online if you want the banter.

## ✨ New

- 🎲 **Make your name.** You name your character at the start now, or roll the dice for a random one if you would rather. Names stay unique, which sets things up nicely for the co-op plans down the road.

Play it free in your browser, desktop or phone, and tell me what still feels off. Plenty more on the way.
